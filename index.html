Rolo Pretzel Turtles

A Perfect balance of Sweet and Salty

By Colin Coleman

Total Time: 25-30minutes

Ingredients

  • 20 square small pretzels
  • 20 Rolo candies
  • 20 pecan halves

Directions

  1. Start of by preheating your oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C) and then line a cookie sheet with parchmennt paper. Gather your ingredients and put them in bowls so that they are easily accessible.

    2. Time: 3-5 minutes

  2. Place the pretzels spaced outon the cookie sheet and then put a Rolo candy on top of each pretzel.

    3. Time: 3-5 minutes

  3. Place your cookie sheet in the preheated oven until the candy softens.

    4. Time:4-5 minutes

  4. Take the cookie sheet out of the oven and place your pecan halves on each of the soften Rolo's and then wait for them to cool.

    5. Time:10-15 minutes

  5. Enjoy your new favoite snack!
